The Bro Signs Story

Bro Signs began in Los Angeles in 2014, in the yard of our founder, Gilberto Molina. With only basic tools and an unwavering drive, Gilberto went door-to-door in Bell Gardens, introducing his craft and building a loyal client base one project at a time.

From those humble beginnings, our commitment to quality paid off. Within a year, we moved into a small office, acquired our first crane, and began taking on larger installations. What started with simple printing services like flyers and banners quickly evolved into full-scale sign-making. One of our biggest breaks came when we partnered with South Central Family Center, which allowed us to take our business to the next level.

Today, Bro Signs is based in Bakersfield, equipped with cranes and a dedicated team to handle both service and installations. Our journey has been one of hard work, innovation, and a dedication to helping businesses stand out. We're proud of our roots and excited for the future as we continue to deliver exceptional signage solutions.

Frequently asked questions

01.

Can you help with the design process, or do I need to provide my own design?

We offer full design services to help bring your ideas to life, but if you already have a design, we're happy to work with it!

02.

What file formats do you accept for printing?

We accept most standard file formats, including PDF, JPEG, PNG, and EPS. If you're unsure, feel free to ask, and we'll help you prepare the right file.

03.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, debit card, checks, and Zelle, apple pay for your convenience.

04.

What types of signs do you offer?

We offer a wide range of custom signs, including storefront signage, vehicle wraps, banners, LED signs, light boxes, and more. We can handle any project, big or small.

05.

What types of print services do you offer?

Our print services include business cards, flyers, posters, brochures, large-format printing, and more. We provide custom solutions to meet all your printing needs.
